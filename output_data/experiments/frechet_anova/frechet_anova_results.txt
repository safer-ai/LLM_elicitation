================================================================================
FRÉCHET ANOVA RESULTS — Beta Distributions with Wasserstein Distance
================================================================================

Method: Fit Beta(α,β) on [0,1] using symmetric confidence interval.
        (min, max) treated as CI with equal tail probs: CDF(lo)=(1-c)/2, CDF(hi)=(1+c)/2.
        Fréchet ANOVA (Dubey & Müller, 2019) with L2-Wasserstein distance.
        Permutation test: 5000 permutations.
        Quantile grid: 201 points.
        Default confidence for missing values: 0.85

Note: Asymptotic p-values (T_n ~ χ²(k-1)) are shown for reference but are
      unreliable at these sample sizes. The permutation p-value is the primary test.
